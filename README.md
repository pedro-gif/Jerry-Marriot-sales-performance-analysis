### Jerry marriot Hotel Bar Sales Performance Analysis May 2024

## Project Overview

This project analyzes sales transactions from Jerry Marriot hotel pool bar to evaluate revenue performance, identify customer purchasing trends, and provide actionable business insights. The analysis was conducted using Microsoft Excel, leveraging data cleaning, Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, and dashboarding techniques to transform raw sales data into meaningful information for management decision-making.

---

## Business Objective

The primary objective of this project is to assess the performance of the hotel's pool bar by answering key business questions such as:

- How much revenue did the pool bar generate?
- Which products contributed the most to sales?
- What are the least performing products?
- Which beverage categories perform best?
- What opportunities exist to improve revenue and customer experience?

---

## Dataset Description

The dataset contains pool bar sales transactions and includes the following information:

## Key Insights

- Heineken single-handedly anchors 8.7% of total bar revenue. 
- Whiskey and Wine combined represent less than 1% of total sales volume
- HENNESSY VS yields the highest revenue-per-unit at ₦130,000 per item. Visa Viz the most expensive drink.
- WATER is the #1 revenue generator at ₦814,000, and closely followed by Heineken.
- Despite lower revenue, Heineken Bar operates at high efficiency



---

## Recommendations

Based on the analysis:

1. Maintain adequate inventory levels for top-performing products.
2. Introduce promotional offers and also prioritize menu visibility for non performing products.
3. To mitigate supply chain risk, build out a secondary premium tier option (like Desperados) to diversify revenue reliance away from a single brand.
4. Freeze all further purchasing for Wine and whiskey purchase and run a "Sunset Cocktail" promotion to liquidate slow-moving premium bottle stock and free up working capital.
